Design - The Art of Communication

Design is the most basic form of communication. It is a way to express yourself, to make a statement and to show off a creative mind. For me, design has always been a creative outlet, imagining new ideas and making digital art that will make people stare in awe. Designing also allowed me to develop an innate aesthetic sense, which elememts will work with whuch font, which colours go together, which is the right design language for this task and so on, which has helped make my work far more appealing and presentable.
